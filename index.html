Welcome to an informative website about Washington state, and its wonderful cities.
Washington State is located in the Pacific North West, on the west coast of the United States.
Click here to visit the official WA State website.

Fun Facts:

  • Capital City is Olympia
  • Ranks 13th in population (about 8 million people)
  • Became a state in 1889
  • 42nd State to be admitted to the Union
  • Nicknamed "The Evergreen State"
  • Home to Microsoft and Boeing
  • Highest point is Mount Ranier at 14,411 feet
  • Demonym is "Washingtonian"
  • Has more than 1000 dams
